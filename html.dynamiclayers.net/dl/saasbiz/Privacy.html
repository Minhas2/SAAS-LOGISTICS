Privacy Policy

Thank you for choosing SAAS LOGISTICS
This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

– Contact details (such as name, email address, phone number)

– Company information

– Geographic location data

– Usage statistics

2.How We Utilize Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

– Delivering and maintaining our services

– Communicating with you

– Enhancing our services

– Protecting the security of our website and services

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. However, we may share it with trusted partners who help us operate our website or provide services to you, as long as they agree to keep the information confidential.

4. SMS Permissions

Your SMS permissions are not shared with any third parties.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of our website.

6. Security

We are committed to safeguarding your personal information and use industry-standard practices to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or loss.

7. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will take effect when the revised policy is posted on our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at info@nexusdispatchservices.com. By using our website and services, you consent to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.
